# Hogam Design Notes

## Visual Identity
- **Warm, naturalistic aesthetic** inspired by traditional Korean hanji paper and earthy materials
- Tiger mascot (호감 = "favorable impression") anchors the brand
- Feels **educational and premium** without being sterile or corporate

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Paper | #F9F8F6 | Base background - warm cream like hanji |
| Ink | #1A1A1A | Primary text |
| Forest | #0D3328 | Primary actions, headings |
| Sage | #E3EAD3 | Secondary accents, backgrounds |
| Persimmon | #F36A2D | Motivation (streaks, CTAs) |
| Tiger | #D7964F | Decorative accents |

## Typography
- **Playfair Display** (serif) for headlines - gives a book-like, aspirational quality
- **Outfit** for body text - clean and modern
- **Gowun Dodum** for Korean text - authentic Hangul representation

## Feel & Personality
- **Rounded and soft** - pill buttons, 4xl card radius
- **Subtle depth** - soft shadows, no harsh contrasts
- **Culturally aware** - hanji texture, dedicated Korean fonts, tiger branding
- **Motivating without being gamey** - streaks and progress feel sophisticated, not childish
- **Organic flow** - FlowingConnector waves between sections, natural transitions

## Current Pages
- **Landing page** - complete, establishes brand
- **Dashboard** - exists with level display, progress cards, memory bank visualization
- **Learn** - study session flow with input, feedback states, progress tracking
